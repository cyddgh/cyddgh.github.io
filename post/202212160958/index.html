Remnote早鸟价将在本月结束

2022-12-16 2 min read

Remnote是一款双链软件，今天收到它的邮件告知早鸟价将在今年结束，明年有新的付费政策（附件1）。它早鸟价规则比较复杂，好像是付费后锁价（至少）1年，具体请阅读附件1的链接。大家感兴趣可以先付款锁价。

我的邀请链接： remnote.com/invite/wGqKQGpJT8WkvyteD

到了年末，一些今年来新出的软件都要退出早鸟价了，例如昨天说的 [[Readwise]] 。你在用的好软件中还有什么软件即将退出早鸟价，欢迎留言。

附件1 Changes To Subscription Plans

Starting Jan 1st 2023, we’re ending Early Bird discounts and adjusting the price of RemNote Pro for new subscriptions. If you have an existing subscription at that time, you will retain your current Early Bird price.

······

We want RemNote Pro to be accessible to students through the new Education discount, so it will remain at $6/month when billed annually or $8/month billed monthly . For others, it will be $8/month billed annually or $10/month billed monthly.

This new pricing goes into effect on Jan 1st, 2023. Existing subscriptions, or subscriptions purchased between now and Jan 1st, will lock in the Early Bird price for at least one year from the date of purchase.

https://feedback.remnote.com/changelog/changes-to-subscription-plans
